About CSE-AI&ML


CSE-AIML (Computer Science and Engineering - Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning) is an undergraduate engineering degree specializing in the design and application of intelligent systems. This 4-year program combines fundamental computer science concepts like operating systems and data structures with in-depth AI/ML topics such as deep learning, natural language processing, and robotics.
